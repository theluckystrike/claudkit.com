❓ Quick FAQ
How is TPM usage calculated? TPM = RPM × avg tokens. Headroom = (limit − usage)/limit × 100%.
What does burst tokens mean? (tier TPM/60) × burst seconds — max tokens in a short burst.
Queue drain time? (N × avg tokens) / (tier TPM/60) seconds, assuming full throughput.
